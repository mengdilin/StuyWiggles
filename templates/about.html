Welcome to Stuy Wiggles!

Constructed by Georgiana Yang, Mengdi Lin, and Victoria Stempel



Purpose:

To serve as a trading floor between students at Stuyvesant High School!

Instructions:

  1. Head to 'Class Information' to create your current schedule. Don't worry if you made a mistake! Just click 'drop'.
  2. For classes that you want, but can't have, click 'Request'
  3. Then, head over to 'Trading Floors' to see what classes are ready for trading!
  4. Disqus is ready for you to discuss away. *Note: We do NOT condone bribery.
  5. Anything wrong with your lunch period? Change in 'Edit Profile'!
  6. Now your Stuy life will be perfect!
  • Remember to checkout 'Graduation Requirements' to make
    • sure you graduate with the perfect schedule!
  • It's on you to make sure your schedule works out!
